## üìù How to Add/Update Newsletter Content

### Step 1: Log into WordPress
1. Go to: `https://stpetelodge139.org/wp/wp-admin`
2. Enter your username and password
3. Click "Log In"

### Step 2: Create a New Newsletter Post
1. On the left sidebar, click **"Posts"** ‚Üí **"Add New"**
2. Enter the newsletter title (Example: "November 2025 Trestle Board")
3. Add your content in the text editor:
   - Type your newsletter content
   - Add headings, paragraphs, lists, etc.
   - Format text (bold, italic, etc.)
   - Add images if needed

### Step 3: Publish the Newsletter
1. When you're done editing, click the **"Publish"** button on the right
2. Confirm by clicking **"Publish"** again
3. That's it! Your content is now live! ‚ú®

## üéØ What Shows on the Website

### Main Newsletter (Featured)
- The **most recent post** you publish will show as the "Latest Edition"
- The full content you typed will display on the page
- The date is automatically pulled from when you published it

### Archive Section
- All **previous posts** (older than the most recent) will show in the archive grid
- Each archived post shows:
  - Title
  - Month/Year (from publish date)
  - Excerpt (first few lines)
  - "Read More" button to view full post

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many newsletters should I create?**
A: Create one post per month. The website will automatically organize them.

**Q: What if I make a mistake?**
A: No problem! Just go back and edit the post. Changes are instant.

**Q: Can I delete old newsletters?**
A: Yes, but it's better to keep them for the archive. If you need to delete one:
- Go to Posts ‚Üí All Posts
- Hover over the post
- Click "Trash"

**Q: Do I need to know any code?**
A: Nope! Just type like you would in Microsoft Word. The editor is very similar.

**Q: What if the website doesn't show my new post?**
A: Make sure:
1. You clicked "Publish" (not "Save Draft")
2. The post status is "Published" (not "Draft" or "Scheduled")
3. Try refreshing the website page (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)

**Q: Can I schedule a post to publish later?**
A: Yes! In the "Publish" box on the right, click "Edit" next to "Publish immediately" and choose your date/time.
