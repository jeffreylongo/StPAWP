What We Look For

Good Character

Men of integrity who strive to be better versions of themselves and contribute positively to society.

Belief in a Supreme Being

While Freemasonry is not a religion, we welcome men of all faiths who believe in a higher power.

Desire to Improve

A genuine interest in self-improvement and helping others along their journey.

Community Minded

Those who want to make a positive difference in their community and the world.

"Freemasonry takes good men and makes them better."
